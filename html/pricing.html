Frequently Asked Questions
What’s my traffic limit? What if I exceed it?

Essential and Premium plans include 500,000 unique visitors per month, while Enterprise plans begin at 500,000 unique visitors. If you exceed your limit, our customer success managers will contact you to discuss a plan that better suits your needs.

What’s a unique visitor?

A unique visitor is a person who views a landing page or Convertable at least once in a given month. Using cookies, we ensure each visitor only counts once towards your traffic limit — no matter how many times they return.

What are “Convertables”?

Convertables are targeted overlays you can launch on top of any web page, each with a dedicated call to action. Customizable triggers and targeting rules give you complete control over who sees your overlays and when, so you can serve the most relevant offers to the right audience.

How does the 30-day free trial work?

You get full access to the Unbounce Platform during your 30-day trial. There are no restrictions, and no obligation to continue beyond your trial. You won’t be charged until your 31st day using Unbounce. To cancel your trial, go to your account dashboard and click “Manage Account.”

What’s the difference between annual and monthly pricing?

Annual plans are billed as a single payment once per year at a discount of 20%. Monthly plans are billed at the beginning of each month, and are not discounted.

Can I change plans?

You can easily change plans via your account dashboard. You’re welcome to upgrade your plan at any time, and downgrade effective at the end of your current billing cycle (either annually or monthly).
